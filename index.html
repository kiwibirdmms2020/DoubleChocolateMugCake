How To Make A Microwave Double Chocolate Mug Cake

Make something tasty!

Ingredient Measurement
Melted butter 2 Tablespoons
Milk 1/4 Cup
Vanilla Extract 1/2 Teaspoon
Egg 1
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder 2 Tablespoons
Self-Rising Flour 1/3 Cup
White Sugar 2 Tablespoons
Brown Sugar 2 Tablespoons
Mini Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips 1/4 Cup
Salt Pinch
Powdered Sugar(optional) No Specific Amount
A small mixing bowl, whisk,spoon, spatula, and 1 or more microwave safe mugs are recommended supplies.

Mixing the Ingredients

1. In a small bowl or the mug, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, salt, and sugars, recommended to be mixed with a whisk.
2. Next, mix in the egg, milk, vanilla extract, and melted butter until completely smooth.
3. Pour the batter into a mug if it was in a bowl.
4. Cook the mug cake for 1 minute in the microwave, if the cake is not fully cooked, microwave the cake in small intervals in a suggested time of 10 seconds.
5. Optional-Sift some powdered sugar on top
